Dorohedoro

In a city so gloomy that everyone calls it the Hole, people live in constant fear. The air and the ground are saturated with magical smoke, and no one is surprised anymore when a little rain suddenly causes passersby to fall in piles or the city cemetery to turn into a battlefield of people against zombies. The magicians, because of whom all this devilry happens, periodically make forays into the Hole and test their tricks on the residents. After one such foray, Nikaido, a corpse collector, finds a huge lifeless body of some poor guy with a crocodile head. Caiman (as Nikaido calls him) manages to survive, but when he wakes up, he realizes that he has lost his memory. Caiman and Nikaido begin to hunt magicians in order to return him to his human form and lost memories.
